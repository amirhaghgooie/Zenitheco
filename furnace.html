Maintenance and Tune-Up

Annual maintenance and tune-up prolongs the lifespan of your heating systems and eliminates need for repair in the future. Our professional technicians will check 22 different points to ensure that your heating performance is optimal. This test includes a camera inspection, carbon monoxide test, and high limit test. At the end, you’ll receive a report card explaining the results.

We do:

  • Camera Inspection
  • Test Thermostat Operation
  • Test HVAC Operation
  • High Limit Test
  • Manifold Pressure Test
  • Carbon Monoxide (co) Test
  • Pressure Switch Test
  • Heat Exchanger
  • Inducer Blower
  • Gas Piping
  • Air Filter
  • Temperature Differential
  • Volts & Amps
  • Venting System
  • Clean Condensate Line
  • Clean Flame Rod Sensor
  • Vacuum Inside & Outside Furnace
