Furnace, Air Conditioners, Indoor Air Quality, and Water Heaters


Zenith Eco Energy Inc. is a fully licensed and insured heating and cooling local business that offers complete HVAC services. We install, service and repair well-known high quality heating and cooling brands and other air quality products. At Zenith Eco Energy Inc., our mission is to be the best in the industry by providing the highest level of reliable customer service and by serving each and every customer professionally, honestly, and fairly. We are here for all your air conditioning, furnace, indoor air quality, and water heater needs, 24/7.

We’re a local, Ottawa-based, heating and cooling company on a mission: to provide the best service ever.

Your home is your oasis. Your furnace, air conditioner, water tank, and air quality matter to you, and so they matter to us. We want to ensure that your services not only work when you need them most—whether that’s during the freezing cold nights of late January, or a sudden July heat wave-- but are eco and wallet-friendly.

Complete Heating and Cooling Services

We provide complete HVAC services from TSSA-certified, and insured, technicians: we sell, install, maintain, repair, service, and protect furnaces, air conditioners, water heaters, HRVs, and other HVAC systems.

We strive to provide excellent service.


What does excellent service mean?

It means that we’re available, on call, 24/7 365 days a year. This even includes holidays. You are our priority, and we are here for you. If you’re having an emergency, we are here to help. Call us at 613-800-9022.

It means that we hire the most qualified, professional technicians. Our technicians are highly skilled and knowledgeable. They will work with you to provide solutions that work for your family’s lifestyle, and budget.

It means great value. You’ve worked hard to earn your money, and we respect that. That’s why we offer you energy-efficient appliances to save money on your energy bills, rebates from the Ontario Power Association, and extremely competitive prices so that you get the most bang for your buck.

It means we, as an organization, believe in three core values: professionalism, reliability, and fairness. All of our staff have been trained to encompass these values.
