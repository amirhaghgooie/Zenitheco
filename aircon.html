Air Conditioner Maintenance and Tune-Up

To best be prepared, we recommend scheduling annual maintenance and tune-up. This keeps your air conditioner efficient, your family cool, and it reduces energy bills and future need for repairs! We will run a complete examination that includes camera inspection and give you a report card explaining your air conditioner’s performance. At Zenith Eco Energy, we believe in educating our customers and we encourage you to ask questions. Our licensed technicians are more than happy to explain what they’re doing and how it’s helping your air conditioner. We have two maintenance plans to suit your needs: Classic Air Conditioner Maintenance and Tune-Up and Premium Air Conditioner Maintenance and Tune-Up.

Classic Air Conditioner Maintenance and Tune-Up

Our Classic Plan includes 22 different inspections, tests, and points of analysis. Our annual Classic Plan keeps your air conditioner healthy and efficient so that you can be comfortably cool, and keep your energy costs down.
