Ottawa Duct Cleaning


We provide air duct cleaning service in Ottawa region. Your heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems all use ducts to transfer air. Air ducts can become filled with dust, mold, and allergens. This not only creates an unhealthy environment but also reduces your HVAC efficiency. Call us today at 613-800-9022 and schedule an appointment for air duct cleaning. Your heating and cooling system will thank you for it. 

What we do:

  • Clean both supply and return air vent leading to all rooms
  • Clean all floor vent and cold air return
  • Clean furnace and inspect the AC coil
  • Clean up to 7 vents 
  • 100% natural fogging disinfectant 
  • Furnace filter replacement or cleaning
  • Inspection using camera
