HVAC Service You Can Trust

We’re a local, Ottawa-based, heating and cooling company on a mission: to provide the best service ever.

Your home is your oasis. Your furnace, air conditioner, water tank, and air quality matter to you, and so they matter to us. We want to ensure that your services not only work when you need them most—whether that’s during the freezing cold nights of late January, or a sudden July heat wave-- but are eco and wallet-friendly.

Complete Heating and Cooling Services

We provide complete HVAC services from TSSA-certified, and insured, technicians: we sell, install, maintain, repair, service, and protect furnaces, air conditioners, water heaters, HRVs, and other HVAC systems.
